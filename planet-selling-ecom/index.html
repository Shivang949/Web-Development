Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and the nearest to the Sun. Mercury is only slightly larger than Earth's Moon. It's the fastest planet, zipping around the Sun every 88 Earth days. Mercury is named for the swiftest of the ancient Roman gods.

Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the sun in our solar system. Also, it is the second smallest planet in our solar system. The possibility of life on mars has aroused the interest of scientists for many years. A major reason for this interest is due to the similarity and proximity of the planet to Earth.

Jupyter

Jupiter has a faint ring system made up of three main parts: an inner halo, a main ring, and an outer gossamer ring. The rings are made of dust, unlike Saturn's rings which are made of ice.
